Analyzing patient waitlists to optimize healthcare resources and minimize delays.

📋 Project Overview

The Medokanja project focuses on analyzing healthcare waiting lists to enhance resource management and improve patient care for a public hospital. The name "Medokanja" combines "Medo" (medical) and "Kanja" (patient in Japanese).


This project aims to equip a hospital with actionable insights to minimize waiting times and enhance operational efficiency. By utilizing historical trends (2018–2021), the dashboard identifies bottlenecks, improves service delivery, and highlights demographic patterns and medical specialties with delays, enabling targeted improvement strategies.

🔑 Key Concepts

🏥 Inpatients

Individuals requiring hospital admission for complex treatment or extended care.

🚪 Outpatients

Receive care without overnight hospitalization.

📊 Statistical Distribution

The dataset is likely not normally distributed, as the mean and median differ significantly, suggesting skewness and potential outliers.

Average

Sensitive to outliers, which can inflate or distort the central tendency.

Median

Provides a more reliable center for skewed data by focusing on the middle value.

Including both average and median in the dashboard offers flexibility to meet diverse analytical needs.
